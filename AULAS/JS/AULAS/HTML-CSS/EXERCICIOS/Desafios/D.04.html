With just 3,300 units produced
and a power of 852 hp
the Dodge Challenger SRT Demon made news
when it was launched in 2018
as one of the fastest series-produced
cars in the world
Equipped with a 6.2 V8 engine supercharged
by a mechanical compressor,
it does the quarter mile (400 meters)
in just 9.65 seconds.
Furthermore,
it goes from zero
to 96 km/h in a mere 2.3 seconds.

The limited-production model is based
on the current-generation Mustang GT500,
but Shelby pulled the supercharger
off the 5.2-liter V8
and replaced it with a pair of turbochargers.
The engine was re-engineered
by Nelson's Racing Engines
and fitted with a dual-feed
fuel system, allowing blends of racing fuel
when needed.
When driving the Code Red using 93-octane gas
from the pump, it will produce over
1000 horsepower and 780 pound-feet of torque.
When using E85, the Code Red's will make
as much as 1300 horsepower
with torque moving up over 1000 pound-feet.

The new ZL1 1LE model received a huge carbon fiber spoiler, new front air deflectors that generate more downforce and exclusive Goodyear Eagle F1 tires. In addition to an automatic suspension system that allows height adjustment at the front, easily adapting to the tracks or the streets and the Active Rev-Matching, a system that equalizes the rotations as soon as the clutch is activated, to optimize the gear changes.
